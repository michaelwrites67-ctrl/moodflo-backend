# Privacy Policy for Moodflo

**Last updated: May 28, 2026**

## Overview

Moodflo is a Chrome extension that provides real-time vocal pattern analysis during Google Meet sessions. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## What We Do Not Collect

Moodflo does **not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
- Audio recordings of any kind
- Meeting transcripts or conversation content
- Video or screen content
- Browsing history or web activity
- Any data from outside of an active Google Meet session

## How It Works

When you activate Moodflo during a Google Meet session, the extension analyses vocal patterns in real time. Raw audio is discarded immediately — it is never recorded or stored. Anonymised acoustic feature data is sent to our secure backend solely to generate real-time suggestions and is not retained after processing.

## Data Storage

Moodflo uses Chrome local storage only to remember your preferences within the extension. No data is synced or shared externally.

## Third Parties

Moodflo uses OpenAI's API to generate suggestions from anonymised acoustic features. No personally identifiable information or audio is shared with OpenAI.
